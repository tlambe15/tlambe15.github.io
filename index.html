Tristan Lambert

I have one cat named Eponine. Generally, I find that I do not like cats.
My cat is the one exception. She's a black cat. I found her about 10 years ago.
She was starving and alone in a dumpster behind a building. I rescued her and
nursed her back to health. She has been the only constants in my life since.

Work-Life

I'm a full time Mother, employee, and student. I am a very busy person. I find myself doing hundreds of tasks every day. This might be overwhelming for most people, but I find the chaos comforting. I always like to keep my mind active and busy. One of my core values is that I am always doing and being better.

My Love for Language

I've been studying linguistics for the past decade. I think that language is one of the most beautiful ways to communicate. Communication, in general, is the most profound thing about life. So far, I've studied the following languages: Spainish, Mandarin, ASL, and Russian.
